About Us

Orbitz is an unapologetic and out-of-this-world beverage brand whose number one priority is to provide the you with a memorable experience through the simple act of choosing a drink. Made for anyone to enjoy, Orbitz ensures a fun and flavour filled adventure in every rocket shaped bottle.

Step into a world of futuristic fun with Orbitz, the drink that turns every sip into a sensory supernova. With its floating bursts of flavor, it’s more than just a beverage: it’s a conversation starter.

History

After many scientific attempts at achieving our iconic zero-gravity orbs and perfecting our many flavours, Orbitz was officially launched in Canada in May of 1996.

Expanding our market beyond just Canada, Orbitz started to be distributed in America in 1997. This led to a sharp increase in popularity, making 1997 our most successful year since launch.

The people of Earth simply weren’t ready for Orbitz’ signature texture and flavours, so in 1999 it was pulled from shelves and taken back to planet Orbitz where it would wait for it’s time to come again.

We’re back! After observing carefully from a distance, the leaders of planet Orbitz determined the people of Earth ready to re-experience the sensory supernova that is Orbitz.

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly are the floating bits?

The floating bits inside Orbitz are small, colorful gel spheres made from food-safe ingredients like gelatin and plant-based gums. They’re specially formulated to stay suspended in the drink instead of sinking or floating to the top.

Is Orbitz a soda?

No, Orbitz isn’t technically a soda, because it isn't fizzy! Orbitz is a non-carbonated fruit-flavored beverage, which means it doesn’t have the fizz or carbonation you’d find in soda. Instead, it’s more like a sweet, flavored drink with those signature floating gel beads suspended inside.

How many flavours are there?

At the moment, there are four vibrant fruit flavours of Orbitz, each inspired by the bold multi-fruit blends of the original drink:

  • Blasting Blueberry
  • Solar Strawberry
  • Orbtial Orange
  • Lunar Lemon
Is ther caffeine in Orbitz?

No, Orbitz is a non-carbonated drink that does not contain any additional stimulants like caffiene, as well as any artifical flavours. Orbitz' caffeine-free nature makes it a fun and refreshing drink you can enjoy any time of day.

Is Orbitz still being made?

Yes! After a very long and boring 20-something years, we are thrilled to be officially back on store shelves! We’ve made several improvements on our original 1996 product, including a new zero-sugar version as well as more straightforward flavour choices, and we’re confident that we’ve made a beverage that is as flavourful as it is fun.
